### Key Questions & Coaching Notes

#### 1. Hiring Manager (Director of Product)
*   *Q:* Cint 2.0 unifies Lucid and Cint. How do you balance feature parity against customer migration risk?
*   *Note:* Testing technical debt trade-offs and client retention strategies.

#### 2. Engineering Lead
*   *Q:* How do we handle transient SSL connection errors or timeouts on the supplier side without polling overhead?
*   *Note:* Testing resilient systems designs (circuit breakers, retries, queues).
