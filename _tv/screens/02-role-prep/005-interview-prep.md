### Key Questions & Coaching Notes

#### 1. Hiring Manager (Director of Product)
*   *Q:* How do you validate and prioritize third-party integrations without creating roadmap bottlenecks?
*   *Note:* Testing partner ecosystem management and release dependencies.

#### 2. Engineering Lead
*   *Q:* What is your strategy for handling third-party API latency or timeouts during synchronous fraud checks?
*   *Note:* Testing technical resilience patterns (circuit breakers, fallbacks).
