This visual board is your career search command center. Run daily searches, analyze matches, and build value-add proposals.

**How to get started:**
1. Configure your criteria in [_config/search-config.md](file:///Users/munron/dev/career-catalyst-icm/_config/search-config.md).
2. Prompt the agent in chat: `"Run a daily job search"`.
3. Check the **Search Status** and **Latest Report** cards on this screen.
